    It

    It is a 2017 American supernatural horror film based on the 1986 novel of the same name by Stephen King. It is the first installment in a two-part adaptation, the second of which, It Chapter Two, was released in 2019.

    Conjuring

    The Conjuring is a 2013 American supernatural horror film directed by James Wan, written by Chad Hayes and Carey W. Hayes. It is the first installment in The Conjuring Universe franchise and stars Vera Farmiga and Patrick Wilson as Ed and Lorraine Warren, paranormal investigators and authors associated with prominent cases of hauntings.

    Insidious

    Insidious is a 2010 American supernatural horror film directed by James Wan, written by Leigh Whannell, and produced by Oren Peli. It is the first installment in the Insidious franchise and stars Patrick Wilson, Rose Byrne, Ty Simpkins, and Lin Shaye. The film follows a family that looks to prevent evil spirits from trapping their comatose child in a realm called The Further.

    Hangover

    The Hangover is a 2009 American comedy film directed by Todd Phillips, who co-wrote the screenplay with Scott Moore and Jon Lucas. The film stars Bradley Cooper, Ed Helms, Zach Galifianakis, Justin Bartha, and Heather Graham.

    Grown Ups

    Grown Ups is a 2010 American comedy film directed by Dennis Dugan, written by Adam Sandler and Fred Wolf, and produced by Sandler's Happy Madison Productions. The film stars Sandler, Kevin James, Chris Rock, David Spade, and Rob Schneider as childhood friends who reunite after their high school basketball coach dies.
