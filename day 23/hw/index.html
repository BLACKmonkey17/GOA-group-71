Shark

Sharks are large, powerful predators that inhabit oceans worldwide. They have a keen sense of smell and can detect blood from miles away.

Dolphin

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex social structures. They communicate using a variety of clicks and whistles.

Octopus

Octopuses are highly intelligent creatures with eight arms lined with sensitive suckers. They are known for their ability to change color and texture to blend into their surroundings.

Turtle

Turtles are reptiles with a hard shell that protects them from predators. They can live both in water and on land, and some species can hold their breath for long periods.

Jellyfish

Jellyfish are gelatinous creatures that drift through the ocean currents. They have tentacles that can sting, and some species are bioluminescent, glowing in the dark.
